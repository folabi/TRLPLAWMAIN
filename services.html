Arbitration

Our approach is one of proffering effective options for our clients with a view to ensuring win-win situations in relationship with their counter parties especially in commercially sensitive and highly specialized transactions. The firm has participated in a number of alternative resolutions and written articles, which are instructive in this regard. Our team has a firm mastery of laws and adhoc rules such as ICC, UNCITRAL model, LCIA that guides the Arbitral process.

Litigation

TRLPLAW prides itself as one of the top-notch firms in Nigeria that have a robust and seasoned Dispute Resolution Team. We provide specialized legal services to high net worth individuals and diverse corporate bodies.

In Litigation, the firm has represented clients in high profile political and commercial cases at the High court, Court of Appeal and apex court. The firm has also been engaged in rendering pro bono services.

Intellectual Property

We have an active intellectual Property protection practice servicing clients in the manufacturing, service, sports and entertainment industries. We undertake and deploy relevant and innovative strategies for our clients to anticipate and prevent the infringement of their rights.

We also enforce our clients' rights through litigation and alternative dispute resolution mechanisms to seek redress in cases where infringements have occurred.

Corporate Law

TRLPLAW is recognized as an undisputed top lead in Global Corporate Practice. What makes our firm one of the most respected is our client base, the mandates we win, our industry expertise, as well as our reputation for creating value for clients and investing in relationships. We work on a broad range of complex transactions for public and private companies, financial institutions, partnerships, non-profit organizations and private individuals.

INFRASTRUCTURE AND PPP

The Infrastructure and PPP sector in Nigeria is an emerging trend, which the firm has been opportune to break into. The firm has rendered legal advise being part of the Transaction Advising (TA) team to a number of MDAs engaged in infrastructure development via PPP Concession agreements ongoing. The firm also doubles as solicitors to one of the Hydro Electric Power Generating Company that emerged as the preferred bidder for the acquisition of Kainji and Jebba power plants.

INTERNATIONAL TRADE

The firm aims to assist companies capitalize on opportunities and minimize on risks associated with international trade. We assist our clients with import compliance, export licensing and control, trade classification and other government agency requirements.

We specialize in trade between the international community and Nigeria.

REAL ESTATES

TRLPLAW Nigeria has two core practice groups: a dispute resolution practice and a transactions practice which is subdivided into banking and finance, civil and criminal litigation, alternative dispute resolution, corporate and commercial law, energy and natural resources, maritime and admiralty, entertainment law, intellectual property, Real Estate and Urbanisation law, infrastructure and PPP law, Employment and Trade Unions law, corporate governance, immigration, securities, project finance and tax.

ENERGY AND NATURAL RESOURCES

The Nigeria Power Sector Reform process has thrown open new vistas of opportunities to private sector participation within the Electric Power industry, which hitherto was a government monopoly.

We have been actively involved since the inception of the process and have acted for several business consortia by providing legal advice, bringing specialist insight and guidance to enable their successful participation in the industry.

FINANCE LAW

Our integrated team of specialists provide sound and innovative service, among others, in handling legal issues relating to banking and finance including but not limited to corporate, structured and project finance and infrastructure financing.

We assist our clients on such issues as investment and divestment, foreign exchange remittance and restrictions, recording and disclosure requirements.
